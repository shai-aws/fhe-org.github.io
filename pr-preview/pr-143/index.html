Welcome

We are a community of researchers and developers interested in advancing Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) and other secure computation techniques. If you want to learn more about FHE, make sure to:

  • Check out the resources page
  • Join the FHE.org community
  • Help us grow the FHE.org community

Here for meetups?

FHE.org hosts regular community meetups featuring presentations from community members on various FHE related topics. Here you can:

  • Register for the next meetup, Towards Practical Transciphering for FHE w/ Setup Independent of Plaintext Space by Jeongeun Park on Feb 8th, 2024 at 4PM CEST

  • Browse resources from all past meetups
  • Submit your own idea for a meetup
