The History of FHE

Since 2009 when Craig Gentry described the first feasible construction for a Fully Homomorphic Encryption scheme using lattice-based cryptography, FHE has grown from a research fascination to a sprawling community of schemes, libraries, companies, and increasing public interest in development of an ecosystem.

Let's take a look at where it came from and how far it has come.

What is FHE?

FHE is the first form of cryptography that enables you to run computations on encrypted data without needing to first decrypt it.
